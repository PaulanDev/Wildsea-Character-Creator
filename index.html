Wildsea Character Creator
This is a non-official website to allow people to make characters for the TTRPG The Wildsea by Felix Isaacs. This is just a little project that I've wanted to make for a long time to practice some basic web dev.

Bloodline

Your bloodline describes what you are, a truth written in blood or sap or chitin. You'll be instantly recognizable as a member of your bloodline, unless you go to impressive length to hide it.

Select 1 Edge, 5 points of Skills/Languages, 2 Resources, 1 Drive, and 1 Mire

Origin

Your origin denotes the kind of environment you lived in before you became a wildsailor. It doesn't need to be where you grew up, though it can be - it could be a place or culture that left a lasting impact on you.
